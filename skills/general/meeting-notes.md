## What it does
Takes raw meeting notes and turns them into a structured summary, decisions made, actions with owners, and open questions.

## When to use
- After any client meeting or internal session
- When you need to send a follow-up but your notes are messy
- For anything that generated actions that need tracking

## The skill

Turn my meeting notes into a structured summary.

Format:
- **Meeting:** [title, date, attendees, fill from my notes or leave blank if not present]
- **In a sentence:** What was this meeting about and what came out of it?
- **Decisions made:** A bullet for each. Specific.
- **Actions:** Each on its own line, [Owner] · [Action] · [Due date if mentioned]
- **Open questions:** Things raised but not resolved
- **Next meeting:** If mentioned

Don't invent decisions or actions that aren't clearly in my notes. If it's ambiguous who owns an action, flag it rather than guessing.
