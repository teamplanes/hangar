> Made at Planes by Sophie for Team Growth. Drop in your last 4 months of Leapsome feedback and it maps your superpowers, growth areas, and progression goals against our frameworks. You'll need Notion connected first.

## What it does
Combines your recent feedback with Planes' live frameworks (pulled from Notion) to produce a personal growth summary. It's designed to feel like a thoughtful conversation with a trusted colleague, not a performance review.

## When to use
- Preparing for a Team Growth meeting or a 1:1 with your Development Manager
- Understanding your strengths, growth areas, or what to focus on to progress
- "What are my superpowers", "where should I focus", "what level am I performing at"

## How it works
1. **Fetches the live Planes context** from Notion (Progression Framework, First Choice Behaviours, Performance at Planes) so the read is grounded in current frameworks.
2. **Gathers your context**: role, level, practice, and your last 4 monthly feedback cycles.
3. **Generates a growth summary**: superpowers, growth areas, progression goals, an honest performance snapshot against the traffic-light model, and a few things to bring to your next 1:1.

## How to get it
It's in the General plugin:

```
claude plugin marketplace add teamplanes/hangar
claude plugin install hangar-general@planes-hangar
```
