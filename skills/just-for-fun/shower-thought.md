## What it does
Takes a half-formed idea or observation and follows it to its interesting or absurd conclusion, useful for pressure-testing assumptions or just enjoying an idea.

## When to use
- When you have a thought you want to poke at
- As a warm-up before a brainstorm
- When you want to see how far an idea holds up before you commit to it

## The skill

I have an idea. Push it as far as it goes.

Here's the idea: [your idea]

Work through it like this:

1. **State the core assumption.** What would have to be true for this idea to be right?
2. **Take it further.** If this is true, what else follows? What else follows from that?
3. **Take it to the extreme.** What does the world look like in 10 years if this idea is completely correct?
4. **The crack.** Where does the logic break down? What's the thing that makes it not quite work?
5. **The useful bit.** Even if the extreme version is wrong, what part of this idea is worth keeping?
