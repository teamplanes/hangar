## What it does
Takes raw progress notes and turns them into a stakeholder-ready update, clear, honest, and properly structured.

## When to use
- End of week or sprint
- Before a steering committee or client check-in
- When you need to communicate project status without scheduling a meeting

## The skill

I'm going to paste my progress notes. Turn them into a stakeholder update.

Format:
- **Status** (one line): Green / Amber / Red with a single reason
- **What's done** (3–5 bullets): specific, outcome-oriented
- **What's next** (3–5 bullets): what happens in the next sprint or period
- **Decisions needed** (if any): what do stakeholders need to decide or unblock?
- **Risks or blockers** (if any): honest, not alarming

Tone: direct and confident. Don't bury risks. Don't over-explain. Write as if the reader has 90 seconds.
